HAWAII TAX KNOWLEDGE

Hawaii has its own rules.
We know every one of them.

Most mainland tax firms don't know Hawaii's General Excise Tax from a sales tax. We've been filing Hawaii returns since 2009 — these aren't surprises to us.

KNOW THIS

Hawaii's GET is not a sales tax

The General Excise Tax applies to all gross business income — services, rentals, wholesale. 4% statewide, 4.5% on Oahu. Most new business owners get this wrong. We make sure you don't.

DEADLINE

Hawaii files April 20 — not April 15

Hawaii's individual tax deadline is 5 days later than federal. Business return deadlines are different again. We keep track of every deadline so you never miss one.

KNOW THIS

Hawaii's top rate is 11% — highest in the US

With 12 income tax brackets, high earners in Hawaii need strategic planning. The difference between good advice and no advice can be thousands annually.

OPPORTUNITY

Hawaii solar credit: 35% state + 30% federal

Hawaii homeowners can offset up to 65% of solar installation costs through tax credits. We make sure you claim this correctly and maximize every dollar.
